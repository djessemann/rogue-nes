# Rogue's Gallery - Game Mechanics: Complete Guide

This is a roguelike dungeon crawler where players descend 26 levels to retrieve the Glowing Goblet and escape.

## Core Progression
Characters begin with 12 HP, strength of 16, and defense of 10. "Your **Max HP increases by 2-9** (random)" upon leveling, along with a full restoration. Experience thresholds follow exponential scaling, requiring 10 XP for level 2 and doubling approximately every two levels until reaching 10,240 XP for level 12.

## Combat System
Attacks use a d20 system where success depends on: "d20 + your level + attack bonus >= monster's armor." Strength provides both hit bonuses (ranging from +0 at 16 to +4 at 21+) and damage bonuses (from +0 to +5). The player's base damage is 1d4 plus modifiers.

## Monster Variety
Fifteen monster types exist, with HP increased 20% compared to earlier versions. Notable threats include Vampires that drain permanent HP, Wraiths that consume experience, and Trolls that regenerate damage. Creatures become progressively stronger deeper underground through scaling mechanics.

## Resource Management
Hunger decreases every turn, with deeper levels causing faster depletion (3 hunger per turn at levels 20-26). Food restores 400 hunger and 25% of maximum HP. The inventory holds up to 23 items including weapons, armor, potions, scrolls, rings, and wands.

## Strategic Elements
Potions and scrolls have randomized appearances, requiring identification before use. Wands offer tactical options like Lightning chains or Fire damage. Players must balance immediate needs against survival preparation while managing increasingly dangerous encounters.
